Diluc Ragnvindr

Born into the affluent Ragnvindr Clan, Diluc is the current owner of the Dawn Winery and a nobleman of high esteem in Mondstadt society. While a past incident caused him to split ways with the Knights of Favonius, he continues to protect Mondstadt in his own way.

Jean Gunnhildr

Jean is the Acting Grand Master of the Knights of Favonius. She is always busy handling unrest across Mondstadt and of course, tirelessly working to maintain the City of Freedom.

Venti

Venti is a free-spirited, wine-loving bard in Mondstadt and the current mortal vessel of Barbatos, the Anemo Archon

Kaeya Alberich

Kaeya is a Cavalry Captain in the Knights of Favonius. He is held in high regard by the people of Mondstadt—even with all his eccentricities and secrets.

Barbara Pegg

Barbara is the deaconess of the Church of Favonius and a self-proclaimed "idol" after learning about them from the intrepid adventurer Alice, Barbara is also the younger sister of Jean.

Amber

Amber is the only remaining Outrider of the Knights of Favonius. She is always ready to help the citizens of Mondstadt—whether it be something simple or perhaps a more challenging task
